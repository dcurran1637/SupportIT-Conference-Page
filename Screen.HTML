Connecting to the display screen and AV equipment

Step 1

Connect the USB cable to your laptop.

The USB-C connector will provide power (65 watts) to your laptop if it supports it. Use this for preference.

You can use the USB-A adapter if your laptop doesn’t have USB-C ports, or it doesn’t work for you using USB-C

Step 2

Power on the display screen with the remote control.

Step 3

Press Win+P to select duplicate or extend display.
